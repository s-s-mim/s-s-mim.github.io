Sentiment Analysis & Modeling

This project is about to classify the customers' reviews (Amazon). This would be helpful for the organization to understand customers' feedbacks. Webportals get a vast amount of feedback from their users. Going through all the feedbacks can be a tedious job. We have to categorize opinions expressed in feedback forums. This can be utilized for a feedback management system. We classify individual reviews and we also determine an overall rating based on individual reviews, so that a company can get a complete idea of the feedback provided by customers and can take care of those particular fields. This makes customers more loyal to the company, which results in an increase in business, fame, brand value, and profits.
